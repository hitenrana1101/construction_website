Meet the partners

Builder Scouts is led by brothers Hardeep and Satnam Singh, offering a unique "office-to-field" management approach that ensures no detail is overlooked

HARDEEP SINGH

Hardeep Singh (Administration & Client Relations): Hardeep is your primary point of contact. He manages the "paper side" of the build—handling project estimates, permits, timelines, and client communication. He ensures your project stays on budget and you are kept informed every step of the way.

Satnam Singh

Satnam Singh (Project Management & Site Operations): Satnam is the "boots on the ground." He oversees all on-site work, managing trades and ensuring that our high standards of craftsmanship and safety are met. His hands-on approach ensures that the physical build matches the architectural vision perfectly.
